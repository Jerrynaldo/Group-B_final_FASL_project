SIERRA LEONE NATIONAL FOOTBALL TEAM

Known as the Leone Stars, representing the beautiful game in Sierra Leone

Our History

The Sierra Leone national football team, commonly known as the Leone Stars, represents Sierra Leone in international football competitions. The team is controlled by the Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA), the governing body for football in Sierra Leone.

Foundation and Early Years

The Leone Stars were formed in 1960, shortly after Sierra Leone gained independence. The team's first international match was played in 1957 against Liberia, marking the beginning of Sierra Leone's journey in international football.

Milestones and Achievements

The Leone Stars have participated in several Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) tournaments, with their most notable appearance being in 1996. The team has also competed in various World Cup qualifying campaigns, showcasing the talent and determination of Sierra Leonean football.

Recent Developments

In recent years, the Leone Stars have experienced a resurgence with the emergence of talented players from both domestic and international leagues. The team has been working towards qualifying for major tournaments and establishing itself as a competitive force in West African football.

Team Structure

Coaching Staff

Our coaching staff is led by experienced professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and tactical expertise to develop the team's potential and implement modern football strategies.

Management

The team is supported by dedicated management professionals who handle logistics, player welfare, and administrative operations to ensure smooth functioning of the national setup.

Support Staff

From physiotherapists to analysts, our support staff plays a crucial role in maintaining player fitness, analyzing performance data, and providing medical support.

Current Squad Philosophy

The Leone Stars squad is built around a core of experienced international players, supplemented by emerging talents from the domestic league and overseas clubs. Our philosophy emphasizes technical skill, tactical discipline, and the fighting spirit that defines Sierra Leonean football.

The team's playing style focuses on combining solid defensive organization with creative attacking play, utilizing the pace and skill of our forwards to create scoring opportunities. We believe in developing players who can compete at the highest level while representing the pride of Sierra Leone.

Future Goals

World Cup Qualification

Our primary goal is to qualify for the FIFA World Cup, bringing Sierra Leone to the global stage of football.

AFCON Success

To return to the Africa Cup of Nations and compete for the title, building on our 1996 achievement.

Youth Development

To establish world-class youth development programs that will produce the next generation of Leone Stars.

Infrastructure

To improve football infrastructure in Sierra Leone, creating better training facilities and stadiums.
