About

Jeremiah Koroma, know as Jerrynaldo is a Sierra Leonean professional footballer who plays as a forward for our national team. Known for his exceptional dribbling skills, clinical finishing, and ability to create chances from seemingly impossible positions, Jerrynaldo has become a key player in our squad since his debut in 2021.

His technical ability and football intelligence make him one of the most dangerous attacking players in international football. Koroma's work ethic and leadership qualities have made him a role model for young players in the national setup.
