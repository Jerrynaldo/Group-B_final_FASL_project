About

Kei Ansu Kamara is a Sierra Leonean professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for our national team. Known for his exceptional vision, precise finishing, and ability to control the tempo of the game, Kamara has become a key player in our squad since his debut in 2019.

His technical ability and football intelligence make him one of the most influential strikers in international football. Kamara's work ethic and leadership qualities have made him a role model for younger players in the national setup.
