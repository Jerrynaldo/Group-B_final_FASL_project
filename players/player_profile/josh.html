About

Josh Koroma is a Sierra Leonean professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for our national team. Known for his exceptional work rate, defensive capabilities, and ability to contribute to both defensive and attacking phases, Koroma has become an integral part of our squad since his debut in 2021.

His technical ability and football intelligence make him one of the most reliable midfielders in international football. Koroma's work ethic and leadership qualities have made him a role model for younger players in the national setup.
