About

Augustus Kargbo is a Sierra Leonean professional footballer who plays as a forward for our national team. Known for his exceptional pace, clinical finishing, and ability to create chances from seemingly impossible positions, Kargbo has become a key player in our squad since his debut in 2019.

His technical ability and football intelligence make him one of the most dangerous attacking players in international football. Kargbo's work ethic and leadership qualities have made him a role model for younger players in the national setup.
