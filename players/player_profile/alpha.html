About

Alpha Turay is a Sierra Leonean professional footballer who plays as a defender for our national team. Known for his exceptional defensive capabilities, aerial prowess, and ability to contribute to both defensive and attacking phases, Turay has become a key player in our defensive setup since his debut in 2019.

His technical ability and football intelligence make him one of the most reliable defenders in international football. Turay's work ethic and leadership qualities have made him a role model for younger players in the national setup.
